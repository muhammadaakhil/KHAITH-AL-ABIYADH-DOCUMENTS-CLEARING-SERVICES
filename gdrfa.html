Learn GDRFA Online Services

GDRFA stands for the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs, which is a government agency in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) responsible for immigration and residency-related services. GDRFA Online Services refer to a range of digital platforms and services offered by the GDRFA, specifically in the emirates of Dubai, to facilitate various immigration and residency-related processes for residents, expatriates, and visitors in the UAE.

GDRFA Online Services allow individuals and businesses to apply for various types of visas and entry permits, such as tourist visas, employment visas, visit visas, and residency permits. Residents and visitors can use these services to renew or extend their visas or entry permits as required. Individuals and companies can sponsor family members or employees through the online platform. This includes applying for family sponsorship, changing sponsors, or cancelling sponsorship. Travelers can check the status of their entry and exit from the UAE, view their entry and exit history, and verify the validity of their visa or entry permit. These services include applying for, renewing, or cancelling residency permits. Residents can also update their personal information or request residency-related certificates.

Amer Centers are service centers where individuals can receive assistance with GDRFA-related transactions. Online services may include the scheduling of appointments at Amer Centers for various services. Entrepreneurs and business owners can access services related to investor visas, partnership contracts, and company amendments. This feature allows users to fill out and submit various application forms online. Users can create and manage their online accounts, make payments for visa-related services, and track the status of their applications. Online platforms typically provide access to customer support and information resources to assist users with their queries and concerns.
