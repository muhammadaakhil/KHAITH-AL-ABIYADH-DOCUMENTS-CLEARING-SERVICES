Learn GDRFA Online Services

The General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA) online services is a platform provided by the United Arab Emirates (UAE) government to manage various immigration and residency-related processes and services online. It is primarily used for visa and residency-related services in the UAE. The GDRFA online services allow residents, employers, and visitors to access and complete a range of immigration-related tasks without the need to visit physical government offices, making the process more efficient and convenient.
