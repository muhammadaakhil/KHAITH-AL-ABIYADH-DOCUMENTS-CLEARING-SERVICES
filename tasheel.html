Learn Tasheel Services

Tasheel Services is a service center network in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) that offers various government-related and administrative services to residents, businesses, and individuals. These centers provide a convenient one-stop solution for a range of services, including government transactions, document processing, and public services. The term "Tasheel" translates to "facilitation" or "simplification," reflecting the aim of these centers to streamline and simplify the process of accessing government services.

Tasheel centers facilitate various government transactions, including visa-related services, labor-related services, and other government-related paperwork. These services often relate to the UAE's labor market, such as labor permits, work contracts, visa applications, and labor card renewals. Tasheel centers assist with the processing of documents and applications, ensuring that they meet the necessary legal and regulatory requirements. This includes attestation, notarization, and verification of documents. Tasheel centers offer typing and data entry services to help individuals and businesses complete application forms and documents accurately.

Some Tasheel centers provide payment services for government fees and charges, allowing customers to settle their financial obligations conveniently at the center. Tasheel centers have trained staff to provide information, guidance, and assistance to customers, helping them navigate the various government procedures and requirements. These centers are often strategically located in urban and residential areas for easy access by the public. Some Tasheel centers also offer online services and appointment booking to further enhance accessibility and efficiency.
